# Council of Ministers <orig>مجلس الوزراء</orig>
## Decision of the Supreme Committee for the Verification of Kuwaiti Nationality <orig>اللجنة العليا لتحقيق الجنسية الكويتية</orig>
### No. (85/10-2) for the year 2025 AD
### Regarding the Loss of Kuwaiti Nationality

Having reviewed the Constitution,
And Article (11) of Amiri Decree No. 15 of 1959 concerning the Kuwaiti Nationality Law <orig>قانون الجنسية الكويتية</orig>, and its amending laws,
And after the approval of the Supreme Committee for the Verification of Kuwaiti Nationality <orig>اللجنة العليا لتحقيق الجنسية الكويتية</orig>,
We have decided the following:

### Article One
Kuwaiti nationality shall be lost by each of the following:

| .7 | Alia Abdullah Mohammed Abdullah Masoud Al-Fuhaid <orig>عالية عبدالله محمد عبدالله مسعود الفهيد</orig> | 179448916 | - |

This is based on Article (11) of the aforementioned Amiri Decree No. 15 of 1959.

### Article Two
The concerned authorities, each within its jurisdiction, shall implement this decision, and it shall be effective from the date of its publication in the Official Gazette <orig>الجريدة الرسمية</orig>.
